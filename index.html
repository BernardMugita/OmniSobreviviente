Our Mission

Omnisobreviviente is a platform which merges different capabilities
from a number of professionals to create a pool of services for
digital marketting. The services include web design, management,
Optimization, Graphic Design, event plannin and music promotion.

Our Vision

Entertainment is becoming a supported art in Kenya's industry
which provides a living to many young upcoming bright minds. Film,
entertainment and sports will blossom in the next few years making
this an influential platform in the years to come.
Our vision is to diversify the quality of print and display media by
giving our clients high quality website, graphic content while also
promoting music and events.
